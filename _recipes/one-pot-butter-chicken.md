## Ingredients

- 6 lb boneless chicken thighs (large cubes)
- 2 large onions, chopped
- 8–10 garlic cloves, minced
- 3” ginger, minced
- 3 green chilies (optional)
- 8–10 ripe tomatoes or 2x 28oz cans crushed
- 1½ tbsp cumin
- 1½ tbsp paprika
- 1½ tbsp Kashmiri chili powder
- 1½ tbsp coriander (optional)
- 1 tbsp turmeric (optional)
- 2½ tsp salt (to taste)
- 8 tbsp ghee (divided)
- 1½ cups water or chicken stock
- 1½ cups heavy cream
- 2 tbsp crushed fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi)
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1½ tbsp garam masala
- 3½ cups (440g) all-purpose flour
- 2 tsp sugar
- 2 tsp instant yeast (or active dry, proofed)
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp plain yogurt
- 3 tbsp oil (veg, avocado, or ghee)
- ¾ cup warm milk
- ¼ cup warm water (add more if needed)
- 4 tbsp melted butter
- 4–5 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp cilantro
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

1. Sauté onions in 6 tbsp ghee until golden. Add garlic, ginger, chilies, cook 2–3 min. Add tomatoes, spices & salt. Cook covered 10–15 min. Add water, blend smooth. Stir in chicken, simmer 25–30 min. Finish with 2 tbsp ghee, cream, fenugreek, sugar & garam masala. Simmer 10–15 min until thick. Taste and adjust. Serve with naan or rice.
2. Homemade Garlic Naan: Mix dry ingredients, then add yogurt, oil, milk, water. Knead until smooth (8–10 min). Let rise in oiled bowl 1–1.5 hrs. Punch down, divide into 8–10 balls, rest 15 min. Heat skillet until very hot. Roll each ball into oval (~¼” thick), cook 1–2 min per side. Brush hot naan with garlic butter. Serve warm.
