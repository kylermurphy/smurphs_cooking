## Ingredients

- 4 skin-on bone-in chicken thighs
- 2 tbsp drizzle olive oil
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 4-5 minced garlic cloves
- zest and juice of 1 large lemon
- salt
- fresh cracked pepper
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1 tbsp oregano
- 1/4-1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 3 tbsp of thick greek yogurt
- handful fresh mint (about 3 tbsp)
- 2 scallions (green and white part)
- 1/2 large jalapeño
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- juice of half a lemon
- salt + pepper
- @kaizenfoodco lowcarb rice
- 1 tomato
- 1/2 onion
- 1 jalapeno
- fresh cilantro
- 1/2 tbsp sumac
- drizzle of olive oil
- lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Grab 4 skin-on bone-in chicken thighs, dab dry with a paper towel and put inside a bowl.
2. To marinade the chicken, grab a medium mixing bowl. Add 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 tbsp tomato paste, 4-5 minced garlic cloves, the zest and juice of 1 large lemon, a couple generous pinches salt, fresh cracked pepper, 1 tbsp smoked paprika, 1 tbsp oregano, and 1/4-1/2 tsp cayenne pepper. Mix really well covering all sides of the chicken. Let sit for 2-6 hours or overnight.
3. Preheat your oven to 410F. Let chicken come up to room temp for about 30 mins.
4. Place chicken on a wire rack and put it in the oven at 410F for 35-40 mins.
5. While your chicken is going, make the sauce: process 3 tbsp of thick greek yogurt, a handful of fresh mint, 2 scallions, 1/2 large jalapeño, 1 tbsp olive oil, the juice of half a lemon, and a couple generous pinches of salt + pepper. Process well, taste, and adjust.
6. Boil the lowcarb rice according to the packaging.
7. For the salad: dice and mix 1 tomato, 1/2 onion, 1 jalapeno, fresh cilantro, 1/2 tbsp sumac, salt, a drizzle of olive oil, and lemon juice.
8. Plate the chicken with your rice, the salad, and sauce!

## Notes

> Uses Kaizen Food Co lowcarb rice as a base.
